Xin Dong | 董鑫

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Yansong Tang. Prior to that, I obtained a Master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Ningxia University, where my research focused on face attribute analysis, including face anti-spoofing and facial age estimation, under the supervision of Prof. Hao Liu. I also hold a Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science at Sichuan University.

Currently, I am passionate about high-fidelity visual generation, including physical 3D reconstruction and human image generation.
