About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate at Tsinghua University supervised by Prof. Yansong Tang. Prior to that, I obtained a Master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Ningxia University, where my research focused on face attribute analysis, including face anti-spoofing and facial age estimation, under the supervision of Prof. Hao Liu. I also hold a Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science at Sichuan University.

Currently, I am passionate about pose-guided human image generation, few-shot image generation, video editing, and tasks related to 3D technology.
